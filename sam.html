S.A.M.

An IoT Project developed to help Monitoring of industrial processes.

About the Project

The Automated Monitoring System (SAM) is designed to facilitate the monitoring and management of processes, ensuring efficient tracking and control. It is particularly relevant for micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises looking to integrate cutting-edge technologies into their operations.

In the context of Industry 4.0, industrial businesses seek to automate processes and monitor production stations to enhance efficiency and competitiveness. Given this scenario, simplifying industrial process monitoring becomes crucial for technological adaptation, growth, and optimization.

To illustrate its application, consider a cookie factory:

1. Cookies are placed in the oven for heating. Once they reach a specific temperature for a set period, an LED light turns on as an indicator.

2. The cookies are then removed from the oven by a conveyor belt controlled by a motor and pass through an ultrasonic motion sensor for tracking.

3. A database is populated with real-time information, including oven temperatures, LED status, and the number of cookies detected by the sensor.
